Deven Philbrick



Biography

Deven Philbrick is a poet living in Ann Arbor, Michigan. His debut collection, Snow Drifts, is forthcoming from Spuyten Duyvil Publishing. His writings have appeared in a variety of literary journals, including BlazeVox Journal, Zone 3, Obsidian, and Palooka. He is a doctoral candidate at the University of Michigan, completing a scholarly dissertation on the topic of 20th century poetry and its relationship to the field of process philosophy.
