Alice



Biography

Alice is a poet based in New England and has spent much of her adult life in the west and east coasts of America. Along with her literary pursuits, she enjoys photography, drawing and painting, ballet, chemistry, and people watching. She can be found going on long walks, conversing with eccentrics, engaging in debate, and frequenting underground events. Alice considers herself a keen observer of the human condition and is on a journey to acquire as much knowledge as possible, embodying the archetypical sage.

Some of her influences include Jim Morrison, W. B. Yeats, William S. Burroughs, Sylvia Plath, Arthur Rimbaud, and William Blake.

Her poetic style is mystical with beat and romantic influences, and hints of occasional satire, romance, and humor. She mainly writes in her journal. Occasionally, she is possessed.
