George J. Farrah



Biography

George J. Farrah has been published widely in journals across the country and internationally. He is the author of the book poetry, The Low Puring Stars, and the chapbook Insomniac Plum, both from Ravenna Press.

He is also an artist regularly exhibiting his paintings nationally.

He received an MFA from Bard College NY. He lives and works in Minneapolis, Minnesota.
