Raymond Neely




Biography

Regional poet Raymond Neely is from the Pipestem area of WV. He attended Concord College where he earned a BA degree in English Composition.  He has been published in Appalachian Journal, ALCA Lines, and Stitches, but also desktop publishes and makes by hand copies of his six chapbooks or collections.
