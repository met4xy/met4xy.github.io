Mark Sonnenfeld



Biography

Marc Sonnenfeld is an experimental writer, collaborator, and publisher thru his Marymark Press label. His work and correspondence are currently being archived at two major American universities. He is active in the international small press scene, having to his credit numerous chap books, broadsides, give-out sheets, and audio collage on cassette and CD.

He is greatly inspired by the Beats and current innovative artforms.

Marc’s work was recently selected for Inside the Outside: An Anthology of Avant-Garde American Poets, edited by Roseanne Ritzema, available through Presa Press at presapress@aol.com
