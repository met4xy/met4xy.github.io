Caleb Puckett



Biography

Caleb Puckett's writing has appeared in a range of publications, including Diode, Mad Hatters’ Review, Moria, NOÖ Journal, and Shampoo. He's published verse chapbooks with Plan B Press, The Feral Press (an imprint of Prehensile Pencil Press) and mgv>2publishing, as well as two book-length prose collections, Tales from the Hinterland and Market Street Exit, with Otoliths. He also edits the online literary journal, Futures Trading (futurestradinglit.weebly.com).
