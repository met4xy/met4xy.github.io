A. Sam More



Biography

The scribbler, the scribblings, and the scribbler's
fictions are, of
course, imaginary.

--adapted from Dostoyevsky's AUTHOR'S NOTE to "Notes from the Underground"

A writer just beginning. Currently on a sabbatical/retreat of sorts in the South Pacific, American Samoa specifically, after being rejected by an MFA program.

Holds a BA with a concentration in Creative Writing: Fiction. Work has appeared in mostly obscure publications, thus far.

You can contact A. Sam More via email at adelaide.s.more@gmail.com
