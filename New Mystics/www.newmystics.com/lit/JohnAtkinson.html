John Atkinson



Biography

John Atkinson has written four published novels, all with Fisher King Press (fisherkingpress.com). Timekeeper, Timekeeper II, Dark Shadows Red Bayou, and Mercy Me.

He has been writing free-lance for 15 years for newspapers and magazines.  His is currently working on a comedy novel that should be finished in the Spring of 2013.
