Marina Boccuzzi


Biography

Marina Boccuzzi was originally born in Middlesex, England but grew up in America. She has spent most of her youth and adult life in Rhode Island but has since moved to Los Angeles where she has spent three years.

Her influences are H.P. Lovecraft, Clive Barker, H.G Wells, Lynda Barry, and Isaac Asimov.

Her writing style is historical horror mixed with macabre influences. She is working on a science fiction children’s story in her spare time and also hopes to finish up the a number of horror stories that can be eventually published in a book.
