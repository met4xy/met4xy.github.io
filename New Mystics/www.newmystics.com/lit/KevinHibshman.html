Kevin Hibshman




Biography

Kevin M. Hibshman has been writing and publishing since 1990 and his poems, interviews and collages have appeared in journals worldwide. In addition to authoring twelve poetry chapbooks, the latest, Incessant Shining, being released by Propaganda Press in 2011, he edited his own poetry magazine, Fearless, for sixteen years. Hibshman received his BA in Liberal Arts from Union Institute and University/Vermont College in 2009. Future plans include a novel and a book celebrating nature spirits.
