Mary Kasimor




Biography

I continue to change as I write. Much of my poetry is about what I have been thinking, or I go on a trip through my unconscious and write about that. I have been published in journals, including moria, GutCult, Otoliths, ab ovo, How2, MIPOesias, and Big Bridge.I have a book of poetry, silk string arias, published by BlazeVox Books. Besides writing poetry, I am a college instructor, and I teach writing and literature classes.
