Amanda Saile


Biography

Twenty-four and jobless, never really been published anywhere until now. I spent my life being second string to a lot of different people, but spending the bulk of it helping everyone. The older of twins, I never knew what I wanted to do growing up. I spent a good part of my life writing to relieve stress and upset. I wrote my first piece at age 6 called My Pumpkin. I still write today, just more for school than for me. I still live at home with my parents, my twin brother, Matthew, and my younger sister, Jessica. I am happily in a relationship with oddly enough a man I met online almost two years ago; we have been together almost a year. I love to express myself in writing.
