Biography
Mark Husk is a Licensed Professional Counselor at United Hospital Center, and Adjunct Faculty for Fairmont State University and Pierpont Community and Technical College.  He lives in Fairmont, WV with his cats, his books, his hiking shoes and his favorite cast iron skillet.
