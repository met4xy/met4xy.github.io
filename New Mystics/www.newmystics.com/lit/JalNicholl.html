Jal Nicholl



Biography

Jal Nicholl lives in Victoria, Australia. His poetry has appeared online in journals including Blackbox Manifold, Cordite, E.ratio, and Otoliths. He is currently preparing his first full-length collection, Oyster Mountain, for publication.
