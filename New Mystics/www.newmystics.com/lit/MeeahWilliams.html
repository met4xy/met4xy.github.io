Meeah Williams



Biography

Meeah Williams is a writer and graphic artist from Brooklyn, NY who’s had work published in many small press literary magazines both online and print. Meeah’s books include two short story collections, Bad Pussy and 101 Sex Positions of the Dead, and two books of poems, The Freak Room and Am I Too Weird for You?. Meeah’s also written two online novels, 13 Doors of X and Geisha in the House of the Dead, which can be accessed via blogger.
