Jason W. Johnson


Biography

Jason W. Johnson grew up on a farm in rural North Carolina.  He received a B.A. in English at The University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill and a M.A. and Ph.D. in English at the The University of Mississippi.  Johnson has published poetry in journals such as Liturgical Credo, Circumambulations, and Barbaric Yawp.  The Anvil’s Children, Johnson’s first collection, will be released this year by Vox Press, Inc.  Johnson lives in High Point, North Carolina and teaches English at Guilford Technical Community College.
